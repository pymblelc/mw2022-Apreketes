What is URecipe

We're here for anyone in concerned about eating disorders and if you need support or wanting to recieve information. URecipe is about providing and meals, dedicated to your unique lifestyle, helping you recover in a safe and caring way.

What is an eating disorder?

An eating disorder is a serious mental illness characterised by disturbances to thoughts, behaviours and attitudes to food and eating.

Signs of an eating disorder

1.Increased preoccupation with body shape, weight and appearance

2.Moodiness or irritability

3.Feelings of being unable to control behaviours around food
