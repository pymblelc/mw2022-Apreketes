WHAT IS URECIPE

We're here for anyone in concerned about eating disorders

and if you need support or wanting to recieve information.

URecipe is about providing and meals, dedicated to your

unique lifestyle, helping you recover in a safe and caring way.

SIGNS OF AN EATING DISORDER

1.Moodiness or irritability

2.Increased preoccupation with body shape, weight & appearance

3.Feelings of being unable to control behaviours around food
